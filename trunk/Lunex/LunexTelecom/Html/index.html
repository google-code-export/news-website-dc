ABOUT US

Lunex Telecom - Leading with Innovation!

Lunex Telecom, founded in 2004, is an FCC licensed international telecom provider based in Atlanta, Georgia. Lunex is the pioneer in pinless calling plans which are progressively replacing traditional international calling cards as the preferred choice for international calling. Our diverse offering of products combined with innovative technologies make Lunex front-runner in the prepaid telecom industry. Find Lunex brands & products online and in retail locations nationwide.

What makes Lunex different?

The answer is simple. Better products and better service. No other telecom provider offers as many diverse calling products, all in one place combined with award winning 24/7 customer and reseller support! Our progressive thinking, innovative technology, and built-in intelligence help us to provide the most convenient & reliable international calling products available, with rates that help everyone stay connected for less!

Our service

Ever tried to get live help with your traditional calling card or voip provider? More than likely you ended up frustrated and still in search of help. If this is you then search no more.
